## 🚀 About Me

Hi, I'm **Sampath Chavali** — an AI/ML enthusiast passionate about building intelligent systems that retrieve, reason, and act.

I'm currently pursuing my **Master's in Information Systems** at the **Robert H. Smith School of Business, University of Maryland, College Park**. My work focuses on **Large Language Models (LLMs)**, **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)**, and **Agentic AI** — building pipelines where AI doesn't just answer, it *does*.

- 🔭 Currently building: **LLM-powered RAG & Agentic AI systems**
- 🌱 Exploring: **LangChain, LlamaIndex, multi-agent orchestration**
- 🎓 Studying: **MS Information Systems @ UMD Smith School of Business**
- 💡 Belief: *The best AI systems don't just predict — they act*
- 🎮 Outside the terminal: Gaming, Music, Trekking & chasing new travel plans
- 📚 Also into: Reading — always have a book on the side

## 🧠 Right Now I'm Deep In...

> **Building intelligent agents that can retrieve, reason, and respond — not just predict.**

Working on RAG-powered systems that combine the precision of retrieval with the fluency of generation, and agentic frameworks where LLMs plan, use tools, and solve complex multi-step tasks end-to-end.
